FAQ

Willy is a minimalist app that gently nudges you a few times a day to pause and take one conscious deep breath — helping you reset your nervous system on the go.

The Willy Technique is a simple breathing method: expand your belly first, then your chest, hold briefly, and exhale slowly. This sequence activates the vagus nerve — your body's natural stress off-switch — creating an immediate sense of calm and wellbeing.

You set your preferred reminder interval — every 30, 60, or 90 minutes — and define your active hours. Willy then sends a gentle notification at each interval. Take one conscious breath, and continue your day. You're always in control of how often and when Willy reminds you.

Throughout the day, stress accumulates silently in our bodies — and the shoulders are one of the first places it shows up. Without realising it, we tend to raise and tense our shoulders as part of the fight-or-flight response, as if bracing for something. Consciously dropping your shoulders sends a direct signal to your nervous system that you are safe. It's a small act with an immediate effect — a physical shortcut to calm that works alongside your breath.
