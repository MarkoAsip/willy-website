Introducing The Willy Technique.

Simple to learn. Easy to remember.

Designed to create an immediate sense of calm.

1. Belly out

Breathe in through your nose. Let your belly fill first.

2. Chest up

Keep breathing in. Let your chest rise.

3. Hold and feel

Pause. Hold. Notice the relaxation.

4. Release

Breathe out slowly through your mouth. Chest first, then belly.
